# Aim Assist Wiki

Welcome to the Aim Assist mod wiki. This mod gives you two tools in PvP:

1. **Aim Assist** — toggled with `J`, locks your crosshair onto the nearest player so you never miss a shot or swing.
2. **Mace Auto-Hit** — when you jump at a player while holding a mace, the mod detects the exact moment you enter smash range and fires the attack for you, maximising the fall-damage bonus.

## Quick Start

1. Install the mod (see [Installation](Installation.md)).
2. Join a world or server.
3. Press **J** — you will see `Aim Assist: ON` in the action bar.
4. Your crosshair now follows the nearest player automatically.
5. Press **;** to open the config screen and turn Mace Mode on or off.
